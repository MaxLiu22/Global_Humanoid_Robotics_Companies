## Criteria

The criteria to be included in the list is as follows:
- The company must be known to develop or manufacture humanoid robotics products or systems.
- Ideally, there should be a public reference to the company's humanoid robotics work. This can be a product announcement, a press release, a video demonstration, official website, social media posts, or public documentation showcasing their humanoid robot models.

**NOTE**: *Companies that only develop components or software for humanoid robots (without developing complete humanoid robot systems) may be considered on a case-by-case basis.*
